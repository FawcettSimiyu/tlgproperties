Our Services

TLG Properties Limited is your go-to partner once you’re ready to buy, sell, lease or rent a home or commercial property. We’ll also take care of your valuations so you get the best value, and property management so you’ll maintain it. For most of us, our home and our business places are at the center of our lives, they’re where we grow together, and where memories are made and kept, so we work with you to ensure they’re the right fit – and investment – for you

Sales & Rentals

Our company is one of Kenya’s leading real estate brokers, has a team of highly qualified, committed Realtors. We have a comprehensive listing of residential and commercial properties for sale and rental.We regularly update our listings and offers a wide selection of properties. Our realtors provide the best advice to assist you on your journey to achieving your real estate goals.

Property Management

Our company provides expert property management services for commercial properties and residential complexes. We manage maintenance budgets and provide advice on property improvements to maintain and increase the value of your building. We make recommendations on how to enhance the aesthetics of your property and to achieve optimal income and return on your investment.

Property Valuation

Whether you are buying or selling your home, accessing a loan, or are acquiring property insurance, you will need a valuation on your property. Our expert team of highly trained, certified Quantity Surveyors both in-house and outsourced possesses a vast knowledge of the market and is ready to meet all your valuation needs on residential, commercial or agricultural properties.

Project Management

We offer construction and technical project management services for commercial and residential projects. Our in-house experts, together with our partners within the industry will ensure that your project is completed on time and within budget guaranteeing customer satisfaction.
